What is hypnotherapy and how can it help you?

Hypnotherapy has been quoted as ‘The best tool in the world for behavioural changes’. It can help to move you forward in your life – in a variety of different areas.

Sometimes you are unaware of what is holding you back, and a hypnotherapy session can help you to find out what is blocking you by accessing your mind on a subconscious level.

You are never put into a trance (like you see on a stage show!) - I am not a theatrical hypnotist performing in front of an audience. I am a therapist and always have your best interest at the forefront of your session.

You are always in control and you will never run around the therapy room bleating like a goat, or squatting to lay an egg! You cannot be made to say or do anything against your will or which goes against your personal values or beliefs. You will be able to relax in my calm and comfortable room, and allow yourself some well-deserved ‘me-time’. It is a very safe therapy and has no side effects.

Whether its anxiety, a fear or phobia, weight concerns, or confidence issues, I can help you move forward. Exam, driving test or interview nerves can hamper you on the day – let me help you prepare and feel more confident in your ability.
